Privacy Policy

We at Supernetworks, Inc. are committed to protecting your privacy, and built SPR to provide people with tools to better protect their network data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect and use information about mobile application users of our Secure Programmable Router.

Information We Collect

Mobile App

We do not collect any personal information about you, such as your name, address, or email address, when you use our app.

Stripe Payment

Supernetworks, Inc. can access the personal data below through our payment service providers. We are not replicating this information elsewhere, and the information is deleted by Stripe after 3 years.

Stripe: Stripe charge ID, expire date, last 4 digits of the card, card type, sender name, origin country and e-mail address.

Third-Party Services

Mobile App

Our app does not use any third-party services that collect or use personal information. We may receive crash logs from Apple which include anonymized code stack traces from where the errors occured.

We do not share customer data with any third-party services.
